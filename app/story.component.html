My story

Over the years, I have tried many different technologies and accumulated experience in a variety of environments. From embedded devices to big financial organizations. Passionate about, but by far not limited to:

  • Development efficiency, agile practises, modern software development processes
  • Continuous integration and Continuous Delivery pipelines (CI&CD) with Jenkins, Travis CI, Gitlab CI
  • Container-based application configuration, testing, deployment and orchestration using Docker, Kubernetes
  • Web services development with Java EE, Java 8, python
  • Front-end development with Angular 2, TypeScript
  • Server administration/upgrade/migration for Version Control Systems (usually to Git), Atlassian JIRA

I always said if I wasn't a developer, I would've been a psychologist or a philosopher. And professionally that helps me dive deeper into relationships with colleagues, purpose of your job, understanding rather than just doing.

I also like to help others so don't hesitate to drop me a message. I can give an advice, share my opinion and experience, or even help you build your next space shuttle.
